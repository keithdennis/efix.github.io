about us

E.fix is an academic support platform that revolves around learning environment with experts across the world who are skilled in writing scholarly arcticles. The platform was formed in 2015 with an aim to assist students, faculty, lecturers in academic writing. The platform has experts who are skilled in Academic writing, Website developement and design as well as Mathematics and Accounting solutions. The platform has assisted over 2500 students and faculty across the globe in Research Proposals and Research Projects.
